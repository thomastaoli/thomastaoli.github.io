Hey there, it's nice to meet you.

My name is Thomas Tao Li. I am a business journalist with Lianhe Zaobao, part of the Chinese Media Group at SPH Media. I recently graduated from Columbia University's Journalism School, where I specialized in Data Journalism. Before my studies at Columbia, I earned a Bachelor's degree in communication from Hong Kong Baptist University and interned at two prominent news agencies in China, Southern Metropolis Daily and Caixin.

This is a collection of my works, ranging from daily local news, business and finance, and human rights stories. My academic and professional projects often utilize quantitative methods and focus on the impact of digital media in journalism. Here, you'll find pieces that represent my dedication to rigorous journalism and my passion for using data to uncover and tell compelling stories.
