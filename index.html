Hey there, it's nice to meet you.

My name is Thomas Tao Li. I'm currently a student at Columbia Graduate School of Journalism majoring in Data Journalism. Prior to Columbia, I gained my Bachelor's degree in communication from HKBU and interned at two news agencies in China, Southern Metropolis Daily and Caixin.

This is a collection of my works, ranging from daily local news, business and finance, and human rights stories. I have also done several research projects in media and communication studies, most focused on quantitative approaches and digital media.
